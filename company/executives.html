Jonas Jonsson
CEO and Founder

Jonas focuses his unique skills in complex entity structure, value identification/creation to disrupting the media space as it enters a time of rapid migration from traditional delivery methods to the internet.

Prior to Ripple, Jonas was Founder of American Standard Television. During his time as AST Jonsson developed an end-to-end IPTV platform delivering HD Over-the-Top video across Asia, a proprietary content management platform, channel programming engine, and full support for multilingual metadata and video-on-demand. He was also co-founder of TaskUS.com proving customer care and back-office support for Fortune 500 Companies. Earlier he was a business analyst ByggVesta and Stellar Holdings. As a series entrepreneur, Jonas builds companies that provides scalable solutions to large problem sets, and combines his business acumen with access to the best software development teams in the world. Jonas also has been granted 2 patents in artificial intelligence and video distribution.
